On the next few pages, you will be asked again to use a mapping to answer questions about the Santa Barbara area.

Please use the map to indicate how much you disagree or agree with the questions. You will be asked to rate each area with a number from -3 to 3,
-3 being strongly disagree, -2 disagree, -1 slightly disagree, 0 neutral, 1 slightly agree, 2 agree, and 3 being strongly agree.
To do this, click on each of the hexagons and rank them when the box pops up.

Navigation:

1) To zoom in and out, use either the plus and minus symbols on the map, or use your mouse scroll button
2) To move the map view, press and hold the left mouse button and move the mouse to drag the map in the desired direction.
